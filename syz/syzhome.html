Welcome!

We are the Phi Chapter of Sigma Psi Zeta Sorority, Inc. at the University of Colorado at Boulder. Our chapter was established on April 24, 2004, and we are the first Asian-interest sorority in Colorado. As SYZters of our national organization and students of the CU campus, we strive to exemplify leadership, activism, and passion.
